Interests

I like...

To read books that challenge my assumptions, educate me on my privilege and help me to grow.

To meet new people; people who share my interests and people who don't. I like people with passion, whatever that passion may be.

To play games with epic storylines and fantastic world building that makes me fall in love with the characters, and sad to say goodbye.

To watch animations with beautiful colour palettes and emotional soundtracks.

To visit new places; busy cities, quiet forests, picturesque towns by the sea - anywhere that I can explore and discover.
